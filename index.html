About the Journey Program

A mission to eradicate fatalities on mine sites.

Every three weeks, someone dies on a mine site in Western Australia. It's totally avoidable, but that's not the worst of it: Globally, someone dies in the primary resources sector every 35 minutes.

We're here to reduce those figures to zero.

The Journey Program is a novel approach to organisational safety management, based on improving the performance and profitability of mine sites everywhere.

Benefit number one Predict the likelihood of a serious incident occurring within in your operation.

Benefit number two is beneficial because of all the benefits it brings you, the reader! So you had better keep reading.

Benefit number THREE Decrease the time it takes your operation to reach an acceptable level of risk.

Focused on performance, not just safety

It's a positive feedback loop: A safe mine is a more productive mine.
