About the Journey Program

A mission to eradicate workplace fatalities.

Every few minutes someone is seriously injured at their workplace in Australia. Ironically, most of the injuries are totally avoidable, but that's not the worst of it: globally, the ILO report 317 million people are serious injured each year!

We're here to reduce those figures.

The Journey Program is a new scientific approach to organisational safety management, based on improving the performance and profitability of workplaces everywhere.

Benefit number one Forecast the likelihood of a serious incident occurring in your organisation.

Benefit number two Our data analysis is specific to your organisation.

Benefit number three Our forescatig will help you assess the likely effectiveness of controls before you put them in place!

Focused on Performance, Not Just Safety

It's a positive feedback loop: A safe workplace is a productive workplace.

Good leaders understand how to manage risk across their organisation. No one wants people to be hurt as it is morally, ethically, finanacially and legally the wrong thing. So why not use safety performance as an indicator of overall business performance? We teach you to protect your people and these sills can be applied to other disciplines. It seems to make sence!

Using the Journey Program teaches your people how to manage risk, and we measure it too!
